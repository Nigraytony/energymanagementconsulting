Our Services

OUR SERVICES

EMCx presents the following list of services for our clients. Additional services can be discussed upon request.

Building Commissioning

New building commissioning is a systematic process to measure, verify, and document that new buildings are designed, installed, operated and maintained according to the owner’s specified requirements.

Retro Commissioning

Retro-Commissioning (RCx) is a systematic process for improving the efficiency and optimization of an existing building’s equipment and systems.


Re Commissioning

Re Commissioning occures when a previously reviewed facility undergoes another round of commissioning, due to a change in ownership or the onset of operational problems.


Facility Assessment

Facility condition assessments allow facility managers to effectively manage assets financially and operationally, assisting in planning for repairs and upgrades

Energy Management

Energy Management allows for the implementation of strategies that adjust and optimize a buildings energy usage.


LEED Administration

LEED is a green build rating system that provides a baseline for the sustanability of a facility.
